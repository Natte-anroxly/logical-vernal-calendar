About The Modes

Northern Mode:
New Year starts at Vernal Equinox (March 19-20)
Southern Mode:
New Year starts at Autumnal Equinox (Sep 22-23)
Universal Mode:
New Year starts at Sidereal Aries (Fixed April 14)
Design Philosophy:
Respects astronomical and cultural differences across Earth

Special Note for Universal Mode

Leap Year Rule:
Uses same Gregorian leap year rules as other modes
April 14th Meaning:
In leap years, April 14th serves as both Leap Day (final day of old year) and March 1 (first day of new year)
Why This Works:
This maintains perfect synchronization with the Gregorian calendar while keeping the simple April 14th anchor date
Example:
In leap year 2024: April 13 = Leap Day = April 14, 441 | April 14 = March 1, 442
